## üìú Our Standards

### ‚úÖ Examples of Behavior that Contributes to A Positive Environment :

- **Being Respectful** : Using Welcoming & Inclusive Language
- **Being Empathetic** : Showing Empathy toward Other Community Members
- **Being Constructive** : Giving & Gracefully Accepting Constructive Feedback
- **Being Accountable** : Accepting Responsibility & Apologizing to Those Affected by Our Mistakes & Learning from the Experience
- **Being Community-Focused** : Focusing on What is Best Not Just for Us As Individuals, But for the Overall Community
- **Being Professional** : Maintaining Professionalism in All Communications & Interactions
- **Being Patient** : Understanding that Contributors have Varying Levels of Experience & Availability
- **Being Collaborative** : Working Together & Helping Others Succeed

### ‚ùå Examples of Unacceptable Behavior :

- **Harassment** : The Use of Sexualized Language / Imagery & Sexual Attention / Advances of Any Kind
- **Trolling** : Trolling, Insulting / Derogatory Comments & Personal / Political Attacks
- **Privacy Violation** : Publishing Others' Private Information, such as a Physical / Email Address, without their Explicit Permission
- **Inappropriate Conduct** : Other Conduct which could reasonably be Considered Inappropriate in a Professional Setting
- **Disrespect** : Dismissing / Attacking Contributors based on their Level of Experience
- **Spam** : Posting Irrelevant Content, Advertisements / Repeatedly Promoting Personal Projects
- **Bad Faith Participation** : Deliberately Derailing Discussions / Refusing to Engage Constructively
- **Intimidation** : Threatening, Intimidating / Otherwise Abusive Behavior toward Any Community Member

## üìã Enforcement Guidelines

Community Leaders will follow these Community Impact Guidelines in determining the Consequences for Any Action they deem in Violation of this Code of Conduct :

### 1. üìù Correction

**Community Impact** : Use of Inappropriate Language / Other Behavior deemed Unprofessional / Unwelcome in the Community.

**Consequence** : A Private, Written Warning from Community Leaders, providing Clarity around the Nature of the Violation & An Explanation of why the Behavior was Inappropriate. A Public Apology may be Requested.

**Example** :

- Minor Inappropriate Comment
- Unintentional Use of Offensive Language
- 1st Time Minor Policy Violation

### 2. ‚ö†Ô∏è Warning

**Community Impact** : A Violation through A Single Incident / Series of Actions.

**Consequence** : A Warning with Consequences for Continued Behavior. No Interaction with the People Involved, Including Unsolicited Interaction with those Enforcing the Code of Conduct, for A Specified Period of Time. This includes Avoiding Interactions in Community Spaces as well as External Channels like Social Media. Violating these Terms may lead to a Temporary / Permanent Ban.

**Example** :

- Repeated Minor Violations
- Dismissive / Disrespectful Responses to Feedback
- Pattern of Unprofessional Behavior
- Ignoring Previous Correction

### 3. üö´ Temporary Ban

**Community Impact** : A Serious Violation of Community Standards, including Sustained Inappropriate Behavior.

**Consequence** : A Temporary Ban from Any Sort of Interaction / Public Communication with the Community for a specified period of time. No Public / Private Interaction with the people involved, including Unsolicited Interaction with those Enforcing the Code of Conduct, is allowed during this period. Violating these Terms may lead to a Permanent Ban.

**Duration** : Typically 30 - 90 Days, Depending on Severity

**Example** :

- Targeted Harassment
- Repeated Violations After Warning
- Deliberate Disruption of Community Activities
- Aggressive / Threatening Behavior

### 4. ‚õî Permanent Ban

**Community Impact** : Demonstrating a Pattern of Violation of Community Standards, including Sustained Inappropriate Behavior, Harassment of An Individual / Aggression toward / Disparagement of Classes of Individuals.

**Consequence** : A Permanent Ban from Any Sort of Public Interaction within the Community.

**Example** :

- Severe Harassment / Threats
- Doxxing / Privacy Violations
- Sustained Pattern of Violations
- Refusal to Comply with Temporary Ban
- Illegal Activities

2. **Provide Context** : Explain Why You Believe the Decision was Incorrect
3. **Review Process** : A Different Community Leader will Review the Case
4. **Final Decision** : The Review Decision is Final

Appeals are Reviewed within 7 Business Days.

## ü§î Frequently Asked Questions

### What if I Witness A Violation?

Report It Immediately Using the Contact Methods listed above. **Do Not** Engage with the Violator / Attempt to Moderate the Situation Yourself.

### What If the Violation involves A Community Leader?

Report It to A Different Community Leader / Directly to the Project Maintainer. If the Violation involves the Project Maintainer, Report It to GitHub via their `Report content` Feature.

### Will My Report be Kept Confidential?

Yes. All Reports are Treated Confidentially. Details will Only be Shared with those Necessary to Investigate & Resolve the Issue.

### What if I made a Mistake?

Everyone makes Mistakes. If You realize You've Violated the Code of Conduct :

1. Acknowledge It Promptly
2. Apologize Sincerely
3. Take Steps to Prevent it from Happening Again

A Genuine Mistake Handled Well demonstrates Good Community Citizenship.

### Can I Discuss My Violation Publicly?

We ask that You respect the Privacy of the Enforcement Process. You may discuss General Principles, But Please Don't Share Specific Details about Ongoing Investigations / Other Community Members involved.
